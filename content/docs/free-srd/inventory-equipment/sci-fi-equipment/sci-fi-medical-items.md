### Altoa Radiation Antidote 5 (ARA-5)

*--Cr — 0lbs — Injector*

Single use injector that removes radiation-based wounds and conditions if applied within 1hr of exposure. Developed by Altoa Research for use by Iris Federation employees only.
{{% /details %}}

{{% details "Hal's Healyglue Patch" %}}
### Emergency Trauma Patch

*--Cr — 0lbs*

A 9×9in patch that closes and sterilizes most lacerations, punctures, and burns while drawing small foreign material such as shrapnel out of the wound. Can also be safely applied directly to a punctured spacesuit, sealing both the wound and the suit. Application costs 2 AP.
{{% /details %}}

{{% details "Pain Suppressant" %}}
### Pain Suppressant

*--Cr — 0lbs — Injector*

Single use injector that dulls pain receptors. Allows character to pause most wound penalties for 1hr. All applicable effects return when pain suppressant effect ends. 1 AP to administer.
{{% /details %}}

{{% details "Vitaboost Injection" %}}
### Vitaboost Injection

*--Cr — 0lbs — Injector*

Single use injector that heals 1 VIT or attribute damage. Does not remove wounds or conditions. Costs 1 AP to administer.
{{% /details %}}
