| Tier | Range (ft/round) | Range (mph) | Examples | Modifier |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Slow** | up to 40 | up to 4.5 | Walking, jogging, mount at walk, vehicle creeping | +0 |
| **Moderate** | 45–120 | 5–13.6 | Running, sprinting, mount at trot/canter, urban vehicle | +4 |
| **Fast** | 125–400 | 14–45 | Mount at gallop, urban/highway car, fleeing vehicle | +8 |
| **Very Fast** | 400+ | 45+ | Highway-plus car, high-speed chase, supersonic aircraft | +12 |

The tier modifier is used as a difficulty adjustment for several speed-related rules — including reaction dodges and impact damage — with a base target of 6 before the modifier is applied. See [Combat](/docs/free-srd/core-rules/combat/) and the vehicle rules for specific applications.
