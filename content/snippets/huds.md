Heads Up Displays, or HUDS, are helmet upgrades that add a display layer to a visor, screen, or lense. They occupy a sublet of the head body slot — Head [HUD] —
and as such can be paired with other helmet upgrades that do n ot occupy the [HUD] slot, such as com units. A HUD can be paired with any kind of helmet that has a visor, lenses, screen, or other viewport that the display system can overlay. Primary hardware and necessary sensors are integrated directly into the helmet, usually including a small camera to intake relative data.
