A character may spend 2 AP to move up to their Speed. The Move action can be taken multiple times in a turn. Some movements may combine multiple modes of travel — such as ground and climb Speeds used in a single move — with the character's maximum travel distance capped at the distance of their Primary Speed.

Move actions provoke Opportunity Attacks when the character enters or leaves the melee reach of an opponent who is able to intercept them.
