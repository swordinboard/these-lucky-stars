Once per round during another character's turn, a character may make an standard Attack against any opponent who takes an aggressive action they are not defending against. This Opportunity Action costs the attacker 1 AP, they must be weilding a melee weapon, and must have already acted in Initiative. Opportunity Attacks cannot normally be made unarmed — see the [Martial Training](/docs/free-srd/character-creation/proficiencies/#martial-training) proficiency for the exception. The defender of the Opportunity Attack must be within melee reach, have perferomed an aggressive action that the attacker is not affected by or the target of, and cannot be moving faster than the Moderate Speed tier (faster than 120 ft/round, see [Speed Tiers](/docs/free-srd/core-rules/action-economy/#speed-tiers)).
