Once per round a character may spend 1 AP to move a single space (5 ft) at any point during their turn. A Step is a minor, deliberate adjustment rather than a full move — a careful reposition, a side-step, or a short deliberate movement.

A Step can only be taken in a movement mode the character has an appropriate Speed for. A character without a swim Speed cannot Step while swimming; a character without a climb Speed cannot Step while climbing.

Step actions do not provoke Opportunity Attacks.
